# Hi there, I'm Jim Beard

**Identity & Access Management Software Developer at Unicon**

With over 24 years of experience in enterprise software development, including nearly 20 years in higher education, I specialize in Identity and Access Management solutions for universities, colleges, and private enterprises. A strong proponent of open source software, I currently focus on Internet2's InCommon Trusted Access Platform, working extensively with [Grouper](https://github.com/Internet2/grouper) and [midPoint](https://github.com/Evolveum/midpoint) to help institutions design, implement, and troubleshoot complex IAM and IGA environments.

As an IAM Software Developer at Unicon, I work with organizations on their identity management and enterprise application challenges:

- Helping organizations evaluate and implement [Grouper](https://github.com/Internet2/grouper) for group and access policy management
- Troubleshooting complex IAM environments for long-standing [Grouper](https://github.com/Internet2/grouper) deployments
- Designing and implementing [midPoint](https://github.com/Evolveum/midpoint) solutions for identity governance
- Supporting institutions through staffing transitions and scaling challenges
- Consulting on InCommon Trusted Access Platform architecture and best practices
- Enhancing enterprise application stacks for private companies focusing on IAM and AWS solutions
